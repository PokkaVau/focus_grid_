About FocusGrid

Welcome to FocusGrid, your personal, secure, and intuitive productivity dashboard. Designed with simplicity and efficiency in mind, FocusGrid helps you structure your daily workflow without the noise of overly complex project management tools.

How Features Work

1. Task Tracker

Manage your daily to-dos effortlessly. You can add tasks, mark them as completed, and filter them by their status (All, Pending, Done). The tracker keeps everything organized in one place.

2. Habit Tracker

Build lasting routines with our Habit Tracker. Add habits you want to cultivate daily. When you complete a habit, check it off! The system automatically tracks your current streak. Remember, streaks reset if you miss a day!

3. Weekly Planner

Plan your entire week at a glance. Our 7-day layout allows you to assign specific tasks to specific days. Click the "+" button under any day to schedule a task.

4. Daily Progress Dashboard

Your efforts are visualized in real-time. The progress bar at the top calculates your daily completion percentage based on the tasks and habits you've finished today. We also provide a new motivational quote to keep you inspired.

5. Daily Reset System

FocusGrid automatically detects when a new day begins. It resets your daily habits so you can check them off again, while safely preserving your ongoing streaks and weekly planner tasks.
